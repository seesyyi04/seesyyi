About me

On an early morning during the summer of 2025, I accidentally dipped my paint brush into my drinking water and realized that I wanted to go into computer graphics.

I'm a first year Master of Science in Computer Science student at the University of Minnesota Twin Cities. I enjoy computer graphics and math. I do some paintings in my free time, like this poppy field background. The math and calculations needed for graphics is interesting to me, but a large part of why I like computer graphics is also that it has a fun, creative visual aspect to it. I like pretty visuals.
