About me

On an early morning during the summer of 2025, I accidentally dipped my paint brush into my drinking water and realized that I wanted develop my career in full stack and computer graphics.

I enjoy learning about full stack because I can see every element in work in the front-end and back-end. Similarily, I can also see the math and coding components working together in any graphics project I do.

In my free time, I do some paintings, like this poppy field background. Developing and incorporating creative elements with technical applications is very fun to me and a large part of why I enjoy full stack and computer graphics. I like pretty visuals.
